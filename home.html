- Questions about our treatments

Click through our Frequently Asked Questions to learn more about ED and how we treat it.

During your first appointment, you will meet with a physician to discuss the issues you are facing, your medical history, and what treatment options would be best for you.

Simply put, ED is the inability to get or maintain an erection. As men age, this becomes increasingly common, and can begin in men as early as in their 30’s.

Erectile dysfunction affects every man differently and can be brought on by a variety of factors, some of which are indicators of another underlying health issue. This is why we approach your sexual health from a total health perspective. Common causes of ED include the following:

  • Constant stress and anxiety
  • Use of tobacco or alcohol
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity
  • High cholesterol
  • High blood pressure

Not every man experiences ED the same way, so why treat it the same? Oakton Primary Care offers a variety of treatment options to help any man experiencing erectile dysfunction.
